A Re-Branding for Guhao Dental Lab

CONTEXT

I have had the pleasure making a new identity for Guhao Dental Laboratory, which offers quality craftsmanship ranging from denture products to orthodontic solutions.

The company is now facing new challenges and opportunities as it incorporates digitalization and 3D printing into the business. Due to its transformation, Guhao Dental Lab needed some design help to create a fresh brand identity, as well as help creating their very first public website, and a mobile app for a thorough inspection and management of the production line.
